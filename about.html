The Story of Evara Crafts

A journey of turning emotions into handcrafted art.

Where It All Began

Evara Crafts was born not from a business plan, but from a quiet obsession with turning emotions into something you can hold.

Founded in 2020 by Shaheena, a self-taught artist and creative entrepreneur, Evara began as a personal exploration of art, calligraphy, and handcrafted storytelling, and slowly grew into a studio trusted to create deeply personal pieces for life’s most meaningful moments.

Over the past five years, Shaheena has experimented across mediums, from portraits and resin to calligraphy, gifting, and custom keepsakes, guided by one belief: art should feel personal, not generic.

With a background in business (MBA) and content writing, she brings not only artistic sensitivity, but also a rare ability to understand people, their stories, their emotions, and what they truly want to express through a piece of art.

Today, every creation at Evara Crafts is still shaped by that same hands-on philosophy: slow, thoughtful, and made with intent.

The Artist

Meet the Founder

Hi, I’m the artist behind Evvara Crafts. For me, creativity has never been a hobby—it has always been a language. A quiet, honest language through which emotions find form.

My journey into art wasn’t shaped by business plans or market trends. It began with an obsession: the desire to preserve moments that would otherwise fade. A handwritten note. A fleeting expression. A feeling too fragile for words alone. I became fascinated with how paper, ink, and texture could hold emotion—how something handmade could carry a heartbeat within it.

Over the years, I immersed myself in calligraphy, mixed media, and handcrafted design. But mastering techniques was only the beginning. What truly transformed my work was understanding the intimacy behind every commission. Every piece is not just created—it is listened to. I believe true luxury lies in personalization. It lies in knowing that what you hold was shaped intentionally, thoughtfully, and exclusively for you.

Evvara Crafts was born from this philosophy. Not to produce art in volume, but to create keepsakes that feel deeply personal. Pieces that live on your walls, rest in your hands, and quietly become part of your story.

When I’m not in the studio, you’ll find me exploring nature for inspiration, studying light and shadow, curating palettes from sunsets, or losing myself in the delicate details of vintage design. These small observations are what shape every brushstroke and every fold of handmade paper.

Art, to me, is not decoration.
It is preservation.
It is memory.
It is feeling made tangible.

With warmth,

Shaheena

How We Create

From Emotion to Art

Roots

Listening

Every creation begins with understanding your story, emotion, and intention. We take the time to hear what this piece truly means.

Branch I

Concept & Composition

We translate feelings into composition, finding the delicate balance and visual tone that accurately captures your emotion.

Branch II

Sketch & Direction

Before the final brushstroke, we outline the intent. Soft sketches and material selections ensure the artwork aligns perfectly with your vision.

Branch III

Crafting & Refinement

Each stroke, texture, and material is shaped by hand with care, evolving slowly until the vision feels honest and complete.

Branch IV

The subtle details that make it yours. From custom framing to personalized seals, every element is curated to honor the emotion it carries.
