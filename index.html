The Artist

The Story Behind Evara

Evara Crafts began as a quiet love for creating things that hold meaning. What started in 2020 as a personal exploration of art, calligraphy, and handcrafted storytelling slowly evolved into a space where emotions are translated into tangible forms.

Founded by Shaheena, a self-taught artist and an MBA graduate, Evara is shaped by both creative intuition and a deep understanding of people. Her journey across multiple art forms, from fine art and portraits to calligraphy, resin work, frames, and curated gifts, reflects an ongoing curiosity to experiment, learn, and refine.

Every piece created at Evara begins with listening. Not just to requirements, but to the story behind the request, the memory, the relationship, the moment someone wants to preserve. This sensitivity to emotion is what defines the brand and sets it apart.

With over five years of hands-on experience, Shaheena brings together traditional craftsmanship and modern aesthetics. Each artwork is designed slowly and intentionally, with attention to detail, material quality, and emotional depth. There are no shortcuts, only patience, process, and purpose.

Evara Crafts today is known for creating deeply personal, made-to-order pieces that feel intimate, thoughtful, and timeless. Whether it’s a portrait, a handwritten note, a framed memory, or a custom gift, every creation is made to feel like it belongs only to the person receiving it.

Because art, at its best, isn’t just seen, it is felt.
